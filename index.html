FAQ

How do I connect my AI model?

Intercept the file in handleFile() and POST to your API. Replace the overlay with your model output or warped garment.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. It’s responsive and supports touch gestures for dragging and resizing.

Can I add more overlays?

Click “+ Glasses” repeatedly or adapt the button to load your catalog. Each overlay is independent.

Is there AR?

The “Try AR” button demonstrates how you’d branch to a WebXR or native app deep link. Replace the handler with your AR experience.

Need customizations?

Swap brand colors, plug your API, or add auth/cart. The template is framework-agnostic.
